Who am I ?

As a DevOps professional, my objective is to support the development and operations teams by implementing and maintaining systems and tools that streamline the software development and deployment processes. The primary goal is to enhance collaboration, automation, and efficiency within the organization. This includes tasks such as configuring and managing infrastructure, deploying and monitoring applications, implementing continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) pipelines, and resolving technical issues that arise. By working closely with both development and operations teams, your aim is to foster a culture of collaboration, agility, and reliable software delivery.
